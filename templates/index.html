BrandAI
Free AI Brand Strategist

BrandAI is a free AI-powered brand strategy tool built specifically for small businesses in India. It was developed as a BCA Major Project.

Most small business owners cannot afford a professional brand consultant. BrandAI solves this by using the power of Groq AI (Llama 3.3 70B) to generate a full, personalized brand strategy in under 15 seconds — completely free.

From brand names and colour palettes to social media captions and competitor analysis, BrandAI gives you everything a ₹50,000 consultant would, at zero cost.

🐍 Python & Flask 🤖 Groq AI 🎨 Bootstrap 5 📄 ReportLab PDF
  • Brand Name Ideas — 3 unique, business-specific name suggestions tailored to your industry and city.
  • Taglines — 3 catchy taglines that match your brand personality.
  • Colour Palette — Primary, secondary and accent colours with psychological reasoning.
  • Brand Story — A compelling, emotional first-person brand narrative written for you.
  • Brand Voice & Tone — Words to use and words to avoid when communicating with customers.
  • Content Ideas — 5 platform-specific social media content ideas ready to post.
  • Marketing Channels — Top 3 recommended platforms with actionable tips.
  • Ready-to-Post Captions — Instagram, WhatsApp Status & Facebook captions with emojis and hashtags.
  • Competitor Analysis — Market gaps, your unique angle, and pricing strategy advice.
  • Font Pairing & Logo Direction — Typography suggestions and logo style guidance.
  • Action Plan — A personalized step-by-step plan for this week, tailored to your goals.
  • PDF Brand Kit — Download your entire strategy as a beautifully formatted PDF.
  • History — All your past strategies are saved and accessible anytime.
  1. 1
    Describe your business Enter your business type (e.g. "Cafe", "Clothing boutique", "Salon") and who your customers are.
  2. 2
    Set your preferences Choose your brand personality (Fun, Premium, Traditional…), your monthly marketing budget, and your city.
  3. 3
    Enter your main goal What do you want most right now? More walk-in customers? Instagram followers? Online sales? Be specific for the best results.
  4. 4
    Click Generate & wait ~15 seconds The AI will craft your complete brand strategy. Scroll through every section, then download your PDF Brand Kit to keep it forever.

💡 Tip: The more specific your inputs, the more personalised your strategy will be. Instead of "shop", write "handmade jewellery boutique in Pune for women aged 25–40."
